Innovation. Implemented

That’s the new buzz word globally.

Today, most of the innovation comes from high-tech labs and research centres. We’d like to see a world where ideas that shape the future come from our kids. After all, they have the most imaginative minds. We’re sure, you agree. And so, we are launching STiTCH - a hackathon that encourages kids to express their innovation through hands-on and interdisciplinary learning.
